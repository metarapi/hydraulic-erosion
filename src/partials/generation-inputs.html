Levels

Highlights control over terrain detail.

Function

Determines the number of noise iterations used in terrain generation, adding layers of detail and complexity.

Impact

  • Higher Values: Enhance terrain detail and complexity, allowing for more intricate features, but increase computational resources and processing time.
  • Lower Values: Create smoother and less detailed terrains, reducing computational load and speeding up simulations.

Recommendation

Adjust based on desired terrain detail and the balance between visual complexity and computational efficiency.

Exponent

Highlights control over terrain roughness.

Function

Adjusts the amplitude scaling factor, influencing the roughness and variability of terrain features.

Impact

  • Higher Values: Increase terrain roughness and variability, leading to more jagged and rugged features.
  • Lower Values: Decrease terrain roughness, resulting in smoother and more gradual terrain transitions.

Recommendation

Use higher exponents for more rugged landscapes and lower exponents for smoother terrains.
